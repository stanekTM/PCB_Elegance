Assign objects to pin
 

Press Assign objects to pin button
Press a
Assign objects to pin

Selected objects will be assigned to a pinnumber (pinname). In the next dialogbox the pinnumber(name) can be selected or edited.

It is possible to assign as many objects as necessary to a pinnumber(name).

The maximum length of a pinnumber(name) is 9 characters.

Press A

Selected objects will be assigned to a pinnumber which equals the previous assigned pinnumber + 1.
