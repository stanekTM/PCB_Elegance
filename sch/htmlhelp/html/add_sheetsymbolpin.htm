Add sheetsymbol pin
 

Press Add pin button
Press a
Add pin

After the function is activated a dialogbox is visible. In this dialogbox one or more pins can be added to the sheetsymbol. The dialogbox consists of three edit boxes and a serie of radiobuttons. The first editbox is the edit box for the labelnames. The second and third editbox is the edit box the incremental placement of the pins. With the serie of radiobuttons the electrical direction can be selected, for all pins that will be added. After filling the dialogbox click OK, and the pin(s) can be placed. To mirror or rotate the pin press the right mouse button.
