Add busconnection

A busconnection is a small symbol that is used to connect a bus with a wire or pinbus.

After the function is activated the busconnection can be placed. The thick part of the busconnection should be placed against the bus, and the smallest part to the wire or pinbus end point.

By pressing the right mouse button the busconnection can be mirrored.
