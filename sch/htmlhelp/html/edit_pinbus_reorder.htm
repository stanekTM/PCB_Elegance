Edit pinbus reorder -> Pinbus

In the next dialogbox the pinbus reorder parameters of a selected components pinbus can be edited.

Pinbus reorder means the sequence of pins will be reordered. Such a pinbus reordering of pins is necessary when pins in this pinbus are swapped. The numbers in a pinbus reorder are index numbers.

For example:

Pinbus:3,9,45,12,41,89,23,63with pinbus reorder 0,1,2,3,4,5,6,7

Pins 12 and 23 should be swapped.

The pinbus reorder would be 0,1,2,6,4,5,3,7 (Index 3 and 6 are swapped)
