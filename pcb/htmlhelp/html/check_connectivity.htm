Check connectivity
 

Sub menu Check -> Connectivity

The connectivity can be checked for all nets, or for one net. The connectivity check for all nets means that all nets (inclusive hidden or disabled) will be checked. When the check is completed all the new calculated connections except for the disabled nets will be made visible. The nets with connectivity errors will be put into a dialogbox.

Also the connectivity of one net can be checked. In the next dialogbox the net can be selected. After pressing the OK button the connectivity for that net will be checked.
