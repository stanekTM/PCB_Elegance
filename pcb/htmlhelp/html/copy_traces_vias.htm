Copy traces/vias

The selected traces/vias of one net will be copied to the desired location. This location will be a pin. Traces/vias will be moved to their new position, when they do not occupy other traces/vias, otherwise vias will be deleted, and traces replaced by a connection. By pressing and keep down the shift key and moving the mouse cursor, the moving center will change.
